Who am I?

Get to know me!

I, Ayrton Hernández, am an electronic engineer and web developer living in Reus, (Tarragona) Spain. I have a degree in Electronic engineering from Universitat Rovira i Virgili. As long as my years as a college student were passing by, my love for web development started to increase. This is the reason why I personally decided to learn everything related to this great world consisting of creating websites and webapps.

I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into simple and attractive interface designs. I also love coding and creating the logic that is behind of any web design.

I'm quite confident, naturally curious, self-taught, and perpertually learning and working on what I love to do: web development.

  • July 2020

Quadcopter by using Arduino.

University Final Project (TFG).
 Universitat Rovira i Virgili. Tarragona. Spain.

This project aims to design the automatic stabilization of a quadcopter controlled remotely. The microcontroller Arduino UNO is used, as well as an android app that connects to a mobile device using Bluetooth technology. Moreover, I describe the hardware and software elements that have been employed, as well as the assembly, programming, and design processes carried out. Finally, the achieved results are presented and the respective improvements are proposed.
